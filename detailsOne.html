If every Big 10 school were a Game of Thrones character

designed for Sherman Ave

In the fall of 2013, one of Sherman Ave's new writers wrote the piece, "If every Big 10 school were a Parks and Rec character," which garnered many views for the site. In May, one of my fellow editors had the idea to continue the theme, only this time with Game of Thrones. As a fan of the show myself, I contributed to some of the character/school pairings and put together images to represent each choice.

My favorite collaboration was Daenerys Targaryen as Indiana University, but I think the most striking is Joffrey Baratheon as Ohio State. And of course, can't look past the Fred and George Weasley additions...
